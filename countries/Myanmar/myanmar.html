Myanmar

Myanmar (Burma) is a country located in Southeastern Asia. It borders 5 other countries including China to the northeast, Laos, and Thailand to the southeast, as well as Bangladesh and India to the northwest. Also, it shares about 1,930 km (1,200 mi) of contiguous coastline with the Andaman Sea and Bay of Bengal. The capital city of Myanmar is Naypyidaw, but its largest city is Yangon (Rangoon) followed by Mandalay.

Population: 54,409,800
