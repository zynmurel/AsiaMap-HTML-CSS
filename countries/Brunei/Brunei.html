Brunei

Brunei (Brunei Darussalam) is a small country on the island of Borneo, completely surrounded by Malaysia. It also shares a coastline with the South China Sea to the north for about 161 kilometers (100 mi). Brunei is split into two non-contiguous sections. First, the western section is more populous and made up of three districts including Belait, Tutong, and Brunei-Muara. Despite having the smallest area, the Brunei-Muara District is the most populous with approximately 70% of people living there.
