India

India is located in southern Asia, along the Arabian Sea and Bay of Bengal. It borders 7 other countries including Afghanistan, Nepal, Pakistan, China, Bhutan, Burma, and Bangladesh. Within the Indian Ocean, it’s near Sri Lanka to the southeast. The country is known for yoga, spirituality, languages, and Bollywood. It’s the second-most populous country in the world, after China. Also, it’s the largest democratic country.

Population: 1,380,004,385

Capital: New Delhi
